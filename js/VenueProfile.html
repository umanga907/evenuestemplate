Washington Room in Olympia Washington

About Space
  • Venue Type: Hotel Meeting Room
  • Room Type: Washington Room
  • Floor Level: 1
  • Room Size: 1386 SqFt

The new decorated Washington Room is a medium size space that can accommodate up to 190 people, depending on setup configuration. The room features all new decor, furnishings, bathrooms and amenities along with vista views of the State Capitol, Capitol Lake, Puget Sound and the Olympic Mountains. Inside or outside catering available. Adjacent rooms also available for larger or breakout meetings. Inquire here.
